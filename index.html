Shared D-8 Framework

Standardising MFT: a shared D-8 framework.

📊 Measure
Common Benchmarks

Adopt ACES as the shared D-8 scoring baseline, enabling cross-border comparability. Align national MFT indices to GMTI methodology for joint rankings and investment cases. Use the RIDA Impact Score (RIS) to measure sustainability, digital readiness, and immersive value.

✅ Certify
Unified Accreditation

Develop a D-8 Halal Tourism Accreditation Mark, mutually recognised across all 8 states, applying CrescentRating standards as the technical backbone. Phased adoption: Malaysia & Indonesia as first movers, remaining 6 states within 3 years. Align with OIC Halal standards + SMIIC norms for global credibility.

🌿 Benefit
Community & Sustainability

Embed community-based tourism requirements — a minimum % of revenue to local communities. Link RIDA's Responsible dimension to SDGs 8, 11, and 12. Establish a heritage preservation fund via tourism levy, and build Muslim women-led enterprise incentives into certification criteria.
